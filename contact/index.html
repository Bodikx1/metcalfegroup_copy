Employment Opportunities

Requirements for becoming a team member are simple – excellent people that are the best at what they do, and are great team players.

Always on our radar are those with a passion for creating new products, a strong, disciplined work ethic, remarkable attention to detail, and are obsessively curious and creative.

Our environment is professional, open-minded, friendly and product-design-oriented. It fosters collaboration and the best innovative thinking.

We look far ahead to what can be, go beyond the call of duty in delivering, and focus on making new innovations real.

Part time opportunities and internships are available periodically which may be a chance for you to demonstrate your exceptional skills.

If you are a mechanical/design engineer, industrial designer, or other product development professional, send your professional resume and portfolio to employment@metcalfegroup.com.

We will give your hard-earned credentials serious consideration for a position on our talented team. Thank you for considering us as an employer.
